Marine Museum at Jobra, Cuttack Museum

The exhibits of the museum give an insight into the ancient Odisha’s famed maritime industry. On display are the tools used by the Sadhabas (or seafarers), age old navigation systems and its evolution.

Odisha State Museum, Bhubaneswar

The Odisha State Museum traces its roots to 1932, initiated by Prof. N.C. Banerjee and Prof. Ghanshyam Dash of Ravenshaw College, Cuttack, gathering archaeological artifacts from diverse locations.

State Tribal Museum, Bhubaneswar

Odisha Crafts Museum, Bhubaneswar

The Odisha Crafts Museum, known as Kalabhoomi, in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India, celebrates the state's artistic heritage. Inaugurated by Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik on March 22, 2018.

More Details
Konark Sun Temple Museum, Konark

The Konark Museum, founded in 1968 and managed by the Archaeological Survey of India, houses 260 architectural remnants and sculptures from the Konark temple complex. Open to the public.

More Details
Regional Science Centre, Bhubaneswar

The Regional Science Centre, opened in Bhubaneswar on September 18, 1989, covers 8 acres. It's part of the National Council of Science Museums, promoting science awareness through displays.
